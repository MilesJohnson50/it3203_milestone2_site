HTTP/3 & QUIC

HTTP/3 runs over QUIC, a UDP-based transport with built-in TLS and multiplexed streams. QUIC avoids TCP head-of-line blocking.

Why QUIC?

  1. Faster 0‑RTT/1‑RTT connection setup.
  2. Packet loss impacts only the affected stream.
  3. Connection migration via Connection IDs.

Developer Notes

HTTP/2 multiplexes over one TCP connection; a lost packet can stall all streams. QUIC isolates loss per‑stream, improving tail latency.
