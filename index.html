Abstract

This site summarizes HTTP semantics and performance from HTTP/1.1 to HTTP/3/QUIC, including methods, status codes, caching, TLS, and transport behavior. Milestone #2 adds a quiz that grades answers immediately on the same page and provides per‑question feedback.
